      Sustainable Fishing Practices Campaign:

      Encouraging local fishermen and communities to adopt sustainable fishing methods to reduce overfishing.

      This campaign is to encourage and educate local fisherman and coastal communities to adopt sustainable fishing methods that protect that protect marine biodiversity as overfishing has become a significant problem in Mauritius.

      To reduce bycatch of juvenile species and endangered marine life, the campaign encourage the use of selective fishing gear that targets adult fishes.

      Fishermen can help prevent the depletion of these species and support the regeneration of fish stock.

      By reducing overfishing and promoting sustainable methods, the campaign aims to ensure that Mauritius rich marine life can continue to support local communities, maintain biodiversity, and contribute to the economy for generations to come.
