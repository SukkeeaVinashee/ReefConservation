      About Us
      Our organisation is heartly delicated for the protection an preservation of Mauritian beautiful marine biodiversity. To ptomote a sustainable future for our oceans, we engage in various conservation efforts activities. By collaborating with local communities, we aim to raise awareness about the importance of preserving our seas as well as to safegard our marine biodiversity.

      Coral Restoration

      We engage in restoring Coral reefs through hands-on activities and through scientific research to rehabilitate places where reefs are damaged.

      We offer educational programs to schools and communities to encourage understanding and awareness of marine conservation efforts.

      Beach clean ups

      We often organize regular beach cleanups. to create a collective effort to protect coastal area, these clean up engage local communities and schools
